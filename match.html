Get ready to match

Time for the first step in your match making, answer the questions and find out how your match is.

1. Which country do you want to visit?

2. How much are you willing to spend?

3. During what period do you want to go on vacation?

4. What does your ultimate vacation look like?

5. What is your personality type (MBTI)?

Remember! The answers you give do not have to be definite. Is your answer not listed? Choose the answer that resembles your answer, you can discuss the planning of your vacation with your match afterwords.
