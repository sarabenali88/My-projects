About this website

This website is built to help people who are lonely or are searching for new friends or those who just want somebody to travel with! So what do you have to do? You can click on 'Register' if you're new here to make an account. After you make an account, you will have to answer questions about your hobbies etc. When you're done with the matching you will get a few options of people who you matched with based on your interests. You can choose someone and start a chat with that person to see if you want to travel together.
